I Love Computers.

Computers have been my interest for as long as I can remember. I started coded quite recently, but I always liked to do other things on my computer, like playing video games. I first discovered computers when I was quite young, fascinated at how they functioned. During the pandemic, I had grown comfortable with computers and found a sense of calm when I was around them. I knew how to work them. Having something so powerful at the click of a button or the opening of a lid was and still is extremely exciting to me.

That's why I coded this website today. To explain why I love computers so much and to show how I can build my entire career out of them.

How it started:

My interest in computers and coding in general came when I was young. I discovered this website called Codecademy which teaches you how to code. I found this free course on Python that would help me get started. I went on to learn how to code receipts and really cool stuff in Python, but then I let go of the hobby as I was 9 at the time and couldn't hold on to something for more than a month. I recently picked up the hobby again as I signed up for a coding class after regaining my interest. Now I was learning a different language: HTML. HTML is Hyper Text Markup Language and is the base of almost every website out there. I made this website with HTML and a CSS library called Bootstrap. It's quite easy to learn, and it allows me to make websites easily for my projects. You may have noticed that almost every single project I've made for I.L.A. has been a website, but that's just because I'm so good at it. It's extremely reliable and allows me to get content out really quickly.
